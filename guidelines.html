General Rules

Exploits/glitches are not allowed. The only exploits allowed are TNT duplication and bedrock breaking.
Autoclickers are allowed for AFK farms. (Mob grinding, fishing, etc.)
Hack clients/mods are strictly prohibited. Clients such as OptiFine are allowed, but clients such as Impact are not. If you are unsure if your client is allowed, DM an admin.
You are allowed a maximum of one alt, for camera/recording purposes. Abuse will lead to punishment. DM an admin for more info.
Do not PvP/troll/grief without consent.
Do not create farms that lag the server.

Spawn Island / Shopping District Rules

Shops must be at least 5 blocks away from roads.
The maximum size of a shop on the main island is roughly 20x20x20. (Flexible as needed)
Do not build a base on the spawn island.
Currency is diamonds.
Prices must be reasonable. For example, do not sell an Elytra for 5 diamonds, or dirt for 10 diamonds.
If you wish to create a shop, DM a staff member with the coords of your desired location and we will mark out your area.
Shops that do not follow these guidelines will be taken down.
